Admissions Process

Step 1: Application

Complete the online application form and submit the necessary documents.

Step 2: Assessment

Attend the entrance exam and interview scheduled by our admissions team.

Step 3: Enrollment

Receive your acceptance letter and complete the enrollment process.

Admission Requirements

  • ✔ Completed application form
  • ✔ Copy of birth certificate
  • ✔ Transcript of records or latest report card
  • ✔ Certificate of Good Moral Character
  • ✔ Two recent 2x2 ID pictures
